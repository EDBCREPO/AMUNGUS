SpaceSus: Operations Manual

Welcome to SpaceSus.io, the high-performance social deduction game. Here, you will learn to survive betrayal or execute the perfect sabotage.

🚀 Game Objective

For the Nauts (Crew)

Your mission is to stabilize the sector and survive.

  1. Complete Tasks: Perform the maintenance duties marked on the map. Every task contributes to the global progress bar.
  2. Report Anomalies: If you find a data trail or a body, press [R] to call an emergency meeting.
  3. Vote: Discuss who the Corruptor is and eject them from the sector.

For the Corruptors (Impostors)

Your mission is to eliminate the Nauts without being detected.

  1. Sabotage: Trigger critical system failures (Oxygen, Lights, Reactor) to win through systemic collapse.
  2. Elimination: Execute Nauts when they are isolated.
  3. Venting: Move through the ducts to travel quickly and establish alibis.
